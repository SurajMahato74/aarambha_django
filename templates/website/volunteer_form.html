Volunteer Application | Aarambha Foundation

Aarambha Foundation is a non-profit, non-governmental organization committed to child welfare, youth empowerment, and community development. Since its establishment, the organization has been driven by the belief that meaningful change begins with collective effort and compassion.

Our volunteer programs aim to connect passionate individuals with impactful opportunities—whether it's supporting educational initiatives, contributing to community projects, mentoring youths, or assisting on the ground during campaigns. Volunteers play a vital role in extending our reach and transforming ideas into real change.

Through projects like the School Dropouts’ Prevention and Re-enrollment Program (SDPRP), Free Tuition Campaign, Rural Library Project, Digital Classroom, Free Health Camps, and motivational seminars, volunteers have touched countless lives across Nepal. Your time, energy, and dedication can help us expand this impact even further.

We believe in teamwork, selflessness, and the power of young minds committed to building a better society. If you share this vision and want to contribute your skills, passion, and time for a meaningful cause, we welcome you to join Aarambha Foundation as a volunteer.

Become a part of our journey—your small effort can create a big difference.
