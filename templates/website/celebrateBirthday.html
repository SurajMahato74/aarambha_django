Celebrate Your Birthday Funding a Big Dream

Your birthday is more than just a celebration—it’s a chance to create joy, hope, and opportunity for someone else. With Aarambha Foundation, you can turn your special occasion into a meaningful act of giving by fundraising for a cause that truly changes lives. Instead of gifts, invite your friends and family to support a project that helps needy children access education and healthcare.

We offer several ongoing projects and programs that you can choose from to donate to or fundraise for. Whether it’s preventing school dropouts, re-enrolling out-of-school children, or supporting a child’s education and health, your birthday fundraiser can give an impactful project the support it needs to grow and succeed.

You can easily share your chosen project’s story through social media, messaging apps, or email, and invite your friends and family to contribute. Every share becomes an opportunity to spread awareness, inspire kindness, and bring more people together for a meaningful cause.

We believe transparency builds trust. That’s why we show clearly how much funding is being raised and how it is being used. As your fundraiser progresses, we will keep you updated on the project’s development and the real-world impact of the support you helped generate.

Celebrate your birthday by giving someone else a reason to dream. Start a birthday fundraiser with Aarambha Foundation and make your special day the beginning of a brighter future for a child.
